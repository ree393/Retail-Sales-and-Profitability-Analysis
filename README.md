# Retail-Sales-and-Profitability-Analysis

Business Problem:
The company has achieved strong sales growth across multiple markets and product categories. However, management has noticed that high sales do not always translate into high profits. 
Some products, regions, and customer segments generate substantial revenue but contribute little or even negative profit.
The leadership team wants to identify the key drivers of profitability, uncover areas where profit is being lost, and recommend actions to improve overall business performance.
